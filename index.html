About us

Fedora Jazz School offers one-to-one tuition for instrumentalists and singers of all ages and abilities. Based at the Barbican Center in the heart of London, we also offer improvisation workshops, and a variety of ensembles with opportunities for students to perform to an audience.

Our teachers are veterans and innovators in the Jazz scene, with years of experience in improvisation, performing, and teaching.


Our teachers work with both adults and children off all ages, and make sure to pair students with the teacher that suits their needs and availability.
